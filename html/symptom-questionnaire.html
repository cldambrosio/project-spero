Introduction

The following part contains questions regarding your overall mental health condition and related symptoms. It also contains sensitive questions which you might be uncomfortable answering. You may opt to stop the test at any point. However, to get more accurate results, it is best to fill all fields. Rest assured that on answering the test, no personal data will be identified and collected. This will be treated as anonymous data.

This questionnaire can help you identify points of inquiry and advise you if you need further assessment and intervention. However, this does not intend to diagnose or give definite cure. Seeking professional advise is highly recommended.

Try not to think hard about the questions. and answer honestly based on your recollection.
